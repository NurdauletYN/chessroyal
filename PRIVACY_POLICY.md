# Privacy Policy

Chess Royal respects your privacy and is committed
to protecting user data.

## Account Information
To use Chess Royal, users are required to create an account.
We collect basic account information such as:
- email address
- account identifier

This information is used only to provide app functionality.

## Payments
Chess Royal offers paid features and subscriptions.
All payments are processed securely through
Apple App Store In-App Purchases.
We do not store or process payment information ourselves.

## Analytics
We may collect anonymous usage data
to improve gameplay and app performance.
This data does not identify individual users.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
